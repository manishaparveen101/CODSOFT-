About Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. Born in Tamil Nadu, India, he grew up in a modest family and studied Metallurgical Engineering at IIT Kharagpur before earning a scholarship to Stanford University. He later completed an MBA at the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

Pichai joined Google in 2004, initially working on the Google Toolbar and later leading the development of Google Chrome. Due to his strong vision and leadership skills, he quickly rose through the ranks, eventually becoming the CEO of Google in 2015 and Alphabet in 2019.

He is known for his calm demeanor, innovation-driven mindset, and humble leadership style, making him one of the most respected leaders in global tech.
